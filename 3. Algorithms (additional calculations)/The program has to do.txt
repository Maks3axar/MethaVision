The program has to do:
- upload images from a microscope
- contrast the image by applying various masks 
- count cells in the optical field
- recognize cell's morphotype: 
	+ monococci 
	+ diplococci
	+ tetracocci
	+ bacilli
	+ long rods
	+ thin rods
	+ small rods
	+ arc cells
	+ double cells
- calculate size of a image's pixel (in µm) from the scale bar (2 and/or 5 µm) reported at the image
- calculate area (in µm^2) for the "shadow" of each recognized cell 
- estimate cell size from the shadow area taking in account the morphotype of the cell
- if the liquid volume between microscope slide and cover glass is fixed and known, then calculate cell concentration in the optical area
- generate report for each image with hystogram and statistics: cell types, cell count, cell size, cell volume, cell surface area, cell concentration
- accumulate and report statistics from batch of images
