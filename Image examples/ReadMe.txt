Example (n=10) images from microscope:
- mixed culture Methylococcus capsulatus (with satellites) - mixed methanotrophic industrial consortium based on the producer strain Methylococcus capsulatus KN2 in continuous bioprocess in bioreactor. Such images were used to train neuronal network. 
- pure culture Methylococcus capsulatus - pure (without satellites) methanotrophic culture of strain Methylococcus capsulatus KN2 in substrate-unlimited batch bioprocess in flask. The example images were taken at 9 [h] in flask#3 of batch growth experiment. Such images were used to analyse population dymanics of pure culture in course of the batch growth.
